Section 1 — PPA Overview & Growth Acceleration
🚀

A PPA Is Not a Discount — It's a Growth Partnership

A Private Pricing Agreement is a contractual commitment where your organization pledges a specific dollar amount of AWS spend over a 1-to-5-year term. In exchange, AWS provides custom pricing benefits across 200+ services. The real value isn't the pricing — it's the predictability, reinvestment capacity, and accelerated cloud adoption that comes with it.

Section 7 — Common Pitfalls & Risk Mitigation

⚠️ Overcommitting Before Optimizing

Locking in a PPA on bloated infrastructure means your commitment reflects waste, not real demand. Post-optimization spend drops and you face true-up fees.

💡 Complete rightsizing, RI/SP coverage, and waste elimination before entering the PPA process.

📈 Aggressive Growth Clauses

Growth clauses with aggressive YoY targets sound great for unlocking better value tiers, but if projected growth doesn't materialize, you're locked into escalating commitments you can't meet.

💡 Model conservative, base, and optimistic scenarios. Commit to the conservative number.

💸 Enterprise Support Cost Surprise

Enterprise Support is mandatory for PPA participation and is calculated as a percentage of monthly usage. At lower spend levels, support fees can consume a significant portion of PPA value.

💡 Factor Enterprise Support into all ROI calculations from day one.

🏢 M&A Implications

PPA commitments survive mergers and acquisitions. The acquiring company may inherit obligations even if the original workloads are decommissioned.

💡 Include M&A flexibility clauses in the contract.

📊 Marketplace Spend Limits

Only up to 25% of your PPA commitment can be satisfied by AWS Marketplace spend. Exceeding this threshold means that spend doesn't count toward your commitment.

💡 Negotiate higher Marketplace inclusion (30-35%) if your stack relies on third-party SaaS.

🔄 True-Up Mechanics

If usage falls short of commitment, a true-up fee is applied at period end. This is a guaranteed minimum spend, not a target — you pay the full amount regardless.

💡 Request quarterly true-up visibility instead of annual surprises. Track pacing monthly.
